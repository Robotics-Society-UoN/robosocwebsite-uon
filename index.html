> Who are we?

RoboSoc is the university's robotics society. We build autonomous robots, run workshops, and compete nationally.

No experience? Perfect. We teach Arduino, CAD, computer vision, and ML for robotics from zero. Weekly build nights, parts provided, mentors on-hand.

Our flagship focus is UniBots — we give every member the teaching, materials and team support needed to build a competition robot.

> All skill levels welcome
> No application process
> Open to all students
